Malvani Chicken

Malvani Chicken Gravy is a rich and flavorful Konkani dish made with a blend of roasted coconut, aromatic spices, and tender chicken, simmered to perfection. It has a bold, spicy taste that's deeply rooted in the traditional cuisine of the Malvan region in Maharashtra.

Prawns Curry

Malvani Prawns Curry is a spicy, coconut-based coastal delicacy made with fresh prawns and a special Malvani masala. It’s known for its bold flavors, featuring a perfect balance of heat and tanginess, typical of the Konkan region’s seafood dishes.

Mackerel Fry

Mackerel Fry, also known as Bangda Fry in the Konkan region, is a crispy and flavorful dish where fresh mackerel is marinated in a spicy masala and shallow-fried to perfection. It's a popular coastal treat, loved for its bold spices and juicy, flaky texture.
